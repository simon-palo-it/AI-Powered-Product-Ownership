About this course

What Is This Course About?

This course equips product professionals to lead modern product discovery with confidence — combining strong product ownership fundamentals with the power of Generative AI. Participants develop product thinking, backlog mastery, and stakeholder skills, while gaining hands-on experience using GitHub Copilot to accelerate and structure product discovery.

Built on Palo IT's Gen-e² methodology, this course bridges timeless product ownership principles with the tools shaping the future of product delivery.

🧠 Gen-e² Methodology

Palo IT's proven framework blending design thinking, agile product ownership, and Generative AI.

🤖 GitHub Copilot for POs

Use AI as a product thinking partner — for discovery workshops, backlogs, and user stories.

Benefits

Why Should You Do It?

  • Further your career by mastering the skills to lead product discovery with Gen-AI — learn to use GitHub Copilot not just as a developer tool, but as a product thinking partner: structuring user needs, drafting Product Goals, generating and refining backlog items, and facilitating discovery workshops faster than ever before.
  • Apply Gen-e² methodology to real product challenges — gain a structured, repeatable approach to product discovery, blending Design Sprint techniques with AI-assisted workflows so you can move from user need to validated backlog faster and with greater confidence.
Learning Objectives

What Will You Learn?

Product Ownership Foundations

  • ✓Articulate the Product Owner's accountability within the Scrum Team and with external stakeholders
  • ✓Understand and apply Design Sprint concepts to support Product Discovery

AI-Powered Product Discovery

  • ✓Apply structured prompting techniques to accelerate product discovery tasks such as persona creation, opportunity mapping, and story writing
  • ✓Use GitHub Copilot as a product discovery tool — generating and iterating on Product Goals, Epics, User Stories, and acceptance criteria with AI assistance
  • ✓Recognise the limitations and risks of AI-assisted product work and apply human judgement to maintain quality and alignment
Who Should Attend

Is This Course For You?

This course is designed for anyone involved in shaping products — whether you're just starting out or looking to sharpen your edge with AI.

This course is for you if you are:

  • A Business Analyst, Project Manager, or Product Manager looking to transition into a Product Owner role
  • A junior or aspiring Product Owner wanting a structured foundation in Scrum and modern product discovery techniques
  • A team lead, founder, or entrepreneur responsible for defining product direction and working closely with development teams
  • Anyone curious about how Generative AI can accelerate product discovery — from ideation to backlog creation
No prior Scrum experience is required. An open mindset and a curiosity for both product thinking and emerging technology is all you need to get started.
