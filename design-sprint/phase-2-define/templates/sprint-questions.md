## What Are Sprint Questions?

Sprint Questions are the critical unknowns or risks that your sprint aims to answer. They are framed as pessimistic assumptions: *"Can we…?"* or *"Will users…?"* questions that, if the answer is "no", would invalidate your direction.

**Example:** *"Will busy parents take time to set up a new app when they already use three others?"*
