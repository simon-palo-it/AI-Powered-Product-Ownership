## Criteria Checklist

A strong problem statement should:

- [ ] Name a specific user or user group
- [ ] Describe a genuine unmet need (not a solution)
- [ ] Be grounded in evidence from Phase 1 research
- [ ] Be narrow enough to address in a sprint
- [ ] Be broad enough to allow creative solutions
- [ ] Avoid prescribing a specific technology or approach
