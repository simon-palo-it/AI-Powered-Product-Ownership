## What Are Lightning Demos?

Lightning Demos are 3-minute presentations where each team member shares an inspiring product, service, or solution from any industry that could inform the sprint challenge. The goal is to broaden thinking and spark ideas before individual sketching begins.

**Rules:**
- Any product or service, in any industry — it does not need to be a direct competitor.
- Focus on one specific feature or interaction that is relevant to your sprint challenge.
- 3 minutes per demo (strictly timed).
- The presenter captures the key insight on a sticky note / shared board.
