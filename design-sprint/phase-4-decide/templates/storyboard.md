## What Is a Storyboard?

The storyboard is a step-by-step map of the selected solution, showing the entire user experience from entry to outcome. It serves as the blueprint for the prototype that will be built and tested.

**Goal:** Create a 10–15 frame story that is detailed enough for anyone to prototype without needing to ask clarifying questions.
